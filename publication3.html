Watricity Business Plan

J. Li, D. Patel, H. Zhang
January 2020

Abstract

The concept of Watricity was born from a common passion for science and technology. After some preliminary research into water wastage, we examined home energy usage and soon realized that a method of monitoring the consumption of both utilities to produce accurate results and then relaying insightful suggestions based on the results, hadn’t existed. Empowered by the opportunity presented to them, we began working on a product that could help citizens become aware of their water and electricity usage. Since Henry has prior research experience in environmental science, Drew in programming, and Jason in financial analysis, the team soon transformed an intangible dream into a real climate change startup. Collaborating across long distances, during school activities, and with limited access to resources, the team faced a variety of challenges when starting up the company. Nonetheless, the greatest hurdle the team faced was conceiving a revolutionary product for consumers wishing to assess the resource consumption of their homes - a calling to combine the team’s strengths and backgrounds in programming, environmental science, and economics. The EnviroAid team firmly holds that a company’s success is driven by unwavering determination, insightful collaboration, and passionate initiative. Our administration is founded upon these principles in our mission to better the environment and promote change for a progressive society.
