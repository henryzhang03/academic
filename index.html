Biography

I’m a high school student at Montgomery High School in Skillman, NJ interested in Physics and Philosophy. I love studying theoretical physics as well as pondering the countless facets of philosophy. More importantly, my goal is to make an impact on modern society. I’ve traveled all over the world, taking in different cultures and meeting people of diverse backgrounds. Much of my time is focused on spreading my love for physics with peers and students.

Aside from my time spent expanding my knowledge, I'm an avid tennis player and classical saxophonist. I don’t know what the future holds, but I hope this website can give a good indication of who I am academically.
